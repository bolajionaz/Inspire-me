About Us

TryAgain Sessions - a social enterprise initiative and drumming school that aims to tutor young people within the local community - and we believe your collaboration would be of great help and support to fulfilling our ethos.

We specialise in teaching drums- introducing the art of drumming to those keen to develop and grow a new musical skill. We believe doing this will be a great benefit to the community by giving the young generation and opportunity to express themselves musically and creatively.

Our incentive is to inspire and motivate young people through music classes, workshops, and conference activities, to prevent them from falling into drugs, alcohol, smoking and any anti-social behaviours.


Over the course of 5 years, we have seen young people emerge from novices to an intermediate level and some experts. We trust that partnering with you would help reach more students in the local community, and give them the necessary resources they need to realise their potential.

The moto behind TryAgain Sessions is 'Never Give Up' and so we aim to:

  • Encourage and Inspire
  • Shine the light of hope
  • Transfer Knowledge
  • Unlock potentials
  • Educate
